
	Operators in C Programming.

	1) Arithmetic Operator.   +,-,/,*   / - it returns Quotient
	2) Assignment Operator.  =,<=,>=,==
	3) Logical Operator.  &&, ||, !
	4) Ternary Operator. ?:
	5) Unary Operator.  ++ (increment by 1), -- (decrement by 1)
	6) Modulus Operator.   %
			    it returns remainder.
